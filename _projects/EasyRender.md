I, together with [Li Bingxuan](https://bingxuan-li.github.io/), presented EasyRender, a scalable, high-performance ray tracing renderer built upon OptiX 8.0, featuring modular architecture, support for pbrt-v3 scenes and Disney Principled Materials. I designed the basic framework and Bingxuan provided implementations of advanced physically based rendering algorithms.

This project was topic of our bachelor thesis. I'd like to thank Bingxuan for tolerating my immature framework design and implementing extraordinary effects!
