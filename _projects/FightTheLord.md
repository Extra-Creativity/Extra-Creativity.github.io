Project in *Practice of Programming in C & C++*. I, together with other two classmates, implemented an strategy that combined human experience, greedy algorithm and DFS. I also implemented a botzone-like local platform so my bots can evolve itself and fine-tune the algorithm parameters.

Surprsingly, my bot still has 1000+ scores (that is, more than initial score 1000) after 5 years. You can check one of the games my bot (Extra_Creativity, the left up corner) played below (or watch on [botzone](https://en.botzone.org.cn/match/69eada5183ee0a54c18430b3)), and I think it behaves better than me since I haven't played this game for many years :smile:.
